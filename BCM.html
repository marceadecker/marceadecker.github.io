BROOKLYN CHILDREN'S MUSEUM

Logo, Branding, Museum Signage

Founded in 1899, The Brooklyn Children's Museum is a children's museum is the first museum in the U.S. that caters specifically to children. I worked with a small team of designers on various iterations of the logo, drawing on the rich history of this bright, playful, yellow museum in the heart of Crown Heights, Brooklyn.
