Howdy, friend!


Born in the middle of winter in a very hot place, I'm an Arizona gal living in Jersey City & working in NYC.

Illustration & Screenprinting is my labor of love. I started a screenprinting/art studio with my fellow design partner George Vazquez called Bad Penny Land, USA. We also print useful and useless pieces of plastic on our 3D printer.

Sometimes I teach/lecture on topics of design & digital humanities. The most recent course I taught was Designing Digital Knowledges at The New School. The students made some pretty fun Twine games that you can play online here.

I like to edit Wikipedia / run edit-a-thons sometimes. You can watch me + a few colleagues talk about it on this recorded livestream, Feminist Encounters with Wikipedia, hosted by the School of Media Studies at The New School.
