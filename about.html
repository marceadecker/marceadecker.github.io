About




Hey, I'm Marcea!

Born in the middle of winter in a very hot place, I'm an Arizonan currently based in the NYC-area. If I'm not designing, I'm probably screenprinting my own artwork under the name Bad Penny Land, USA. Sometimes I teach/lecture on topics of design & digital humanities; the most recent course I taught was Designing Digital Knowledges at The New School.

I'm a proud dog-mom to a grumpy schnauzer named Ollie, and I love coffee and punk rock music.
