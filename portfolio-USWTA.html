U.S. Wildlife Trafficking Alliance

The United States Wildlife Trafficking Alliance (USWTA) is a voluntary coalition of non-profit organizations, companies, foundations and media interests that will be working closely with the U.S. government in a private/public partnership to reduce the purchase and sale of illegal wildlife and wildlife products.

The work I did for U.S. Wildlife Trafficking Alliance spans rebranding (logo, visual identity, style guide, event signage), social media campaigns, and web design. The logo itself does not single out any animal in particular, but rather represents the many species illegally trafficked every day. The human hand symbolizes the responsibility we have in protecting wildlife.
